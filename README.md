# TATA Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Retail Sales Dashboard built using Power BI.  
The objective is to analyze sales performance, revenue trends, top customers, and global demand distribution.

---

## Key KPIs
- Total Quantity Sold: 5M
- Total Revenue: 9.73M
- Total Unit Price: 2.49M

---

## Key Insights

1. Monthly Revenue Trend
- Revenue increased steadily from September to November.
- November recorded the highest revenue (1.5M).
- A sharp decline is observed in December (0.4M).

2. Top Customers by Revenue
- Customer ID 14646 generated the highest revenue (0.28M).
- Top 3 customers contribute a major portion of total revenue.

3. Country Performance
- Netherlands and EIRE lead in total sales.
- Germany and France are strong European markets.
- Australia shows consistent demand.

4. Global Demand
- High demand in Europe and North America.
- Moderate contribution from Asia-Pacific region.
